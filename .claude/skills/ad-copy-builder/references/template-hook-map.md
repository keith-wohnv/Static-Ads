backbone for Primary Text. Pick the hook that best matches the specific image's visual message.

## Hook Fill-in Reference (examples)

Use these filled examples as inspiration for the tone and specificity level. Replace with your brand's details:

**The Open Loop** → `"I spent 30 days testing every [product category] on the market — here's what most companies won't tell you"`

**The Testimonial Hook** → `"I noticed a difference in the first week. By month 2, I was hooked." — Sarah M., verified customer`

**The Price Reframe** → `"You spend $8 on coffee every morning. [Your product] costs less. And actually changes your [outcome]."`

**The Number Shock** → `"50,000+ customers have already made the switch — here's why the number keeps growing"`

**The Versus** → `"Other [competitors]: $X–$Y/mo, hidden fees, hassle. [Your brand]: Starting at $Z/mo. Everything included."`

**The Root Cause** → `"We don't just treat the symptoms. We fix the root cause of [problem]."`

**The Cost of Inaction** → `"Every month you wait, [problem] is costing you [specific losses]. This is fixable."`
